    FAQ

    Good to know before you book

    Can't find what you're looking for? Get in touch — we're happy to help.

    How does the booking process work? +

    Send us an enquiry, and a travel designer replies within one business day with a tailored proposal. Once you're happy, a deposit secures your dates and we build out the full itinerary.

    What is your cancellation policy? +

    Deposits are fully refundable up to 45 days before departure. Between 45 and 14 days, 50% is refundable, and inside 14 days bookings are non-refundable — which is exactly why we recommend travel insurance.

    Which payment methods do you accept? +

    All major credit and debit cards, bank transfer, and most digital wallets. Larger trips can be split into interest-free installments on request.

    Can I customize a package? +

    Absolutely. Every itinerary is a starting point — extend your stay, swap hotels, add experiences, or start from a completely blank page.
